Returns and Replacements:

Our policy does not allow for general returns.

We prioritize the quality and integrity of our products.
However, in the following exceptional circumstances, we are committed to facilitating replacements if you inform us within 7 days of receiving the order:

                • Incorrect products delivered

                • Missing products in the order

                • Receipt of damaged products

                • Delivery of expired products

Conditions for Non-Acceptance of Returns or Exchanges:
However, there are certain conditions under which we will not be able to accept returns or process exchanges:

                • Opened, used, or altered products.

                • Missing original packaging elements such as mono cartons, labels, etc.

                • Return or replacement requests generated three days after product delivery.

                • Reporting damaged or missing products two days after product delivery

To request a return/replacement Choose either of the following methods:

                • Within 7 days of receiving the product, place a return/exchange request.

                • Drop an email to wholesomenutritech@gmail.com, including the Order ID, registered mobile number, and reason for return/exchange.

To Initiate return/replacement request please follow the steps: 

                • Provide Video or Photo Proof: For any issues, kindly share a video or photo proof to help us
                assess the product's quality and expedite your request.

                • Allow two working days for us to review your return request.

                • Pickup Process: Once your return request is approved, we will arrange for our courier partner
                to pick up the delivered products.

                • Verification and Replacement/Refund Initiation: Upon receiving your returned product(s), we
                will verify it against the claim and proceed with either replacement or refund based on stock
                availability.

                • Refund Processing: Once your return is successfully processed, your refund will be initiated.
                Refunds should reflect in your original mode of payment within 6-7 working days.
                Our customer happiness team may require additional information before processing your
                replacement request. Once approved, a new order ID for the replacement will be generated and can
                be viewed in your order history.
