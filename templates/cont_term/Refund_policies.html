Order Cancellation, Refund, and Return Policy

At Vittzios, we understand that circumstances may arise that require you to cancel your order. We have designed a straightforward order cancellation process to ensure your convenience. Please refer to the following guidelines to cancel your order for Health Gummies:

Cancellation Process:

You can initiate the order cancellation process by following these steps:

Online Platform:

Visit our official website and navigate to the "My Orders" page within your account. Here, you will find a list of your orders, and against each applicable order, a cancellation button will be displayed.

Cancellation Timing:

You have the flexibility to request the cancellation of your order at any time prior to the scheduled delivery.

Cancellation Procedure:

Locate the order you wish to cancel and click on the provided cancellation button.

For orders that have not yet been delivered, the cancellation process will be seamless, and no further action is required from your end.

Refund Process:

For prepaid orders, the refund process varies based on the status of your order:

If the order is in the "Confirmed" status, you can opt for either of the following refund methods:

  • Refund to your original payment source, which might take 5-7 days to reflect.

For orders that have not yet been delivered (statuses prior to delivery), the refund will be processed directly to your original payment source.

In the event that your order has been shipped (identified by statuses such as 'Shipped' or 'In-transit'), cancelling the order will trigger a notification to our logistics partner. The return process will be initiated within 24 hours of cancellation. Refunds will be initiated only upon the confirmation of the return process.

We appreciate your understanding of our order cancellation policy for Health Gummies. Should you have any questions or require assistance, our customer support team is available to guide you through the process. Thank you for choosing Vittzios for your health and wellness needs.

Cancellation and Refund Status Communication:

Upon successful cancellation of your order, you will receive a notification via WhatsApp/SMS confirming the cancellation.

When a refund reference ID is generated by our payment partner, you will be notified via WhatsApp/SMS. Refunds are typically credited to your original payment source within 5-7 days after this notification.

Returns and Replacements:

Our policy does not allow for general returns.

We prioritize the quality and integrity of our products.

However, in the following exceptional circumstances, we are committed to facilitating replacements if you inform us within 7 days of receiving the order:

  • Incorrect products delivered
  • Missing products in the order
  • Receipt of damaged products
  • Delivery of expired products

Conditions for Non-Acceptance of Returns or Exchanges:

However, there are certain conditions under which we will not be able to accept returns or process exchanges:

  • Opened, used, or altered products.
  • Missing original packaging elements such as mono cartons, labels, etc.
  • Return or replacement requests generated three days after product delivery.
  • Reporting damaged or missing products two days after product delivery

To request a return/replacement choose either of the following methods:

  • Within 7 days of receiving the product, place a return/exchange request.
  • Drop an email to wholesomenutritech@gmail.com, including the Order ID, registered mobile number, and reason for return/exchange.

To Initiate return/replacement request please follow the steps:

  • Provide Video or Photo Proof: For any issues, kindly share a video or photo proof to help us assess the product's quality and expedite your request.
  • Allow two working days for us to review your return request.
  • Pickup Process: Once your return request is approved, we will arrange for our courier partner to pick up the delivered products.
  • Verification and Replacement/Refund Initiation: Upon receiving your returned product(s), we will verify it against the claim and proceed with either replacement or refund based on stock availability.
  • Refund Processing: Once your return is successfully processed, your refund will be initiated. Refunds should reflect in your original mode of payment within 6-7 working days.

Our customer happiness team may require additional information before processing your replacement request. Once approved, a new order ID for the replacement will be generated and can be viewed in your order history.

Miscellaneous:

In rare cases of payment partner network failures leading to the absence of a refund reference ID, please reach out to us on wholesomenutritech@gmail.com. We will diligently investigate the matter, and if the circumstances are valid, we will provide a separate link within 24 hours for you to claim the refund.

Contact Information:

For any inquiries or clarifications regarding our Cancellation, Refund, and Return Policy, please don't hesitate to reach out to us on wholesomenutritech@gmail.com.
